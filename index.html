Acquisition & Updates

Notice: Automation infrastructure is currently under development. All setups, purchases, and page updates are processed manually via tickets.

  1. Join our communications server: discord.gg/msQzHwfaeE
  2. Open a support ticket in the designated channel.
  3. To buy: State your intended tier and transmit payment via Crypto or Robux. Then, provide your HTML files.
  4. To update: Simply drop your new HTML files into your ticket and request an update.
